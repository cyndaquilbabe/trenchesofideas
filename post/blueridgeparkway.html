Those Misty Bountains Blue

Driving along the Blue Ridge Parkway near the end of fall is always an adventure in more than just scenic wonders. I mean there's... leafless trees here, leafless trees there. Some highly suspicious fog. And no one around for miles but you!

This trip was just between my aunt and I this time and it was short but pretty. There were still some leaves falling and twirling on their way down along the roads. This trip was mostly for picture taking and I think I'm going to revisit it when the leaves aren't nearly all gone. Maybe a bit closer to the beginning of fall rather than the end, huh.
