Relic From the Gilded Age

So I've discovered that rich people are really extravagant and in particular the Vanderbilts. I mean, their "vacation home" was bigger than my house! And the christmas tree was taller than two of my dad stacked on top of eachother!

Anyway, the park as a whole is utterly beautiful. It's got scenic views, a river running behind the mansion, and the mansion is curently undergoing restoration of the outside. Me and my mom went during fall/winter so the trees were mostly bare, but the ground was still covered in orange and red leaves. So, I mean, it was utterly beautiful and the gardens were stunning to take pictures of.

Mostly, I can't wait to see if I can go back sometime late-Spring or early summer when the garden and greenhouses are in full bloom.
