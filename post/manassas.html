Grass, Grass Everywhere

And more than a few cannon sightings too if you're looking in the right places along Manassas Battlefield. I mean, there was a really cool bridge that I almost slipped and fel on a rock into a stream tryng to get pictures of too.

See, I like going to and visiting battlefields, but not when it's exceedingly cold, okay. Look, I'm barely 5'4", I don't generate heat well at all. Regardless of my misery in the cold, Manassas was pretty. mostly grass, a couple crossed wood fences, the occasional grouping of greening cannons.

Anyway, there was this gigantic old tree at Manassas, like it was easily taller than the Visitor Center it was next to. There's nothing incredibly important about the tree to be honest, but it's one of the first things I think of whenever I'm trying to think about the battlefield. The other thing was this bridge a little ways away from the main field. there was a dirt path going down a hill so people could get to the stream/river/whatever you call it underneath and take pictures there.

The bridge was just a plain stone and dirt bridge, but the stones and tree branches underneath were pretty - I say most of this stuff is pretty don't I? Anyway, Manassas is nice, bit boring, but it's fun if you bring someone along to make fun of things with.
