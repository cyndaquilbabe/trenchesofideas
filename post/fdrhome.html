Let's Find a President

So me and my mom went on an adventure and found this really nice house in Hyde Park, NY. I mean you could almost mistake it as really important for some reason. Also SD cards hate me for some reason, yay!

The worst thing to happen was something damaging my SD cards halfway to taking pictures and having no idea how to get the pictures I took off of both cards. So I ended up buying a new SD card from a CVS and onto FDR's Home we went!

Like most of the parks I've visited in the last few years, it was in that weird space between Fall and Winter, but it was also on the same day that I visited the Vanderbilt Mansion. I think the funniest part of this trip was seeing the horse stables where there was a stall named "New Deal." I mean, what a Roosevelt thing to do. The views around the park are breathtaking and it's sort of funny how part of the view of the river was obscured because of Roosevelt's own experiments in reforestation.
