The Patriot Trails

When in Valley Forge... Why was there a statue with spurs on his boots? Which house was the Marquis de Lafayette's? What's up with the giant arch? I have so many questions, America explain!

No, but in all seriousness, Valley Forge is pretty nice for a scenic drive. I don't really recommend going there if you're going to be in the car for a while longer after driving through, because you're going to be cranky as all get out. Personally the highlight of my trip, was trying to figure out which house was the Marquis de Lafayette's. All four of us in the truck - my mom, older sister, grandmother, and myself - got into the debate on which it would have been.

My visit to Valley Forge was in the middle of a trip between Rhode Island and Maryland. I wish I could go again when I'm in a better mood - take more pictures and be less of a brat about taking pictures of things my mom wants pictures of.
